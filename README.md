The history directory has historical information on QR to give Claude some background.

The presentation Claude created on the history of QR and how LLMs are being used in QR research today is in the top folder or you can ]directly view the Google Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1RKJUGHEsSNN25vZ_25p8oaMIV9IqoehJ6_GGI2u8NP4/edit?usp=sharing)
